Creamy Chicken Torillia Soup

This will be the best soup you have ever tasted. It is very easy to make and highly modifyable. You could choose to make it in a crock pot as well. Choose your spice level. The more cayanne pepper, hot sauce, and jalapeno, the more spicy it will be. Its perfect blend of Texas and Mexico!



Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoons butter
  • 1 small yellow onion, diced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, diced
  • 1/2 Red sweet pepper, diced
  • 1/2 Orange sweet pepper, diced
  • Garlic powder
  • Adobo seasoning
  • Onion powder
  • 2 Tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 15 oz. Mexican corn can, drained
  • 1 10 oz. can Rotel diced tomatoes with green chilies, undrained
  • 1 15 oz. can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 5 cups chicken broth
  • 2 small boneless skinless chicken breasts, or 2 cups shredded chicken
  • 1 pinch Cayenne Pepper
  • Tablespoons of Tony's Cajun Seasoning (or season to taste
  • 1-2 teaspoons hot sauce
  • 1 packet Siete Mild Taco Seasoning
  • 1 ½ cups cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1/3 cup cream cheese, softened
  • Dices Avacado or Guacamole
  • Sour cream
  • Tortilla Chips
  • cilantro

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken breast with tony's cajun seasoning, onion powder, and adobo season. Sprinkle evenin on both sides. Cook the chicken how you like. Bake or boil. Do not let it dry out. Cook until the inside is while.
  2. After the chicken is done and cool, shred it how ever you prefer.

    3. Note: If you purchased shreded chicken, skip these first two steps.

  3. Heat 2 Tbsp. butter over medium heat and add the diced onions and peppers. Sauté for 4 minutesor until soft. add 1 teaspoon of garlic powder,Tony's cajun seasoning cook, and adobo seasoning while cooking.
  4. Add all remaining ingredients EXCEPT for the cheddar cheese, cream cheese, and garnish ingredients. If using another brand of toco seasoning, start with 2 tablespoons and add more to taste. Be careful not to make it too salty.
  5. Let the soup gradually come to a gentle bubble, partially covered. Keep the temperature medium low so the chicken does not get touch. It needs to stay juicy.
  6. Reduce heat to low and gradually sprinkle in the shredded cheese and softened cream cheese. Stir until smooth and creamy.

    7. Note: Make sure the base isn't too hot when the cheese is added or the dairy will separate and the consistency will be grainy.

  7. Taste and add any additional seasonings as needed. Add some to a bowl. Add Garnishs on top (Avacado/guacamole, sour cream, torillis chips, and cliantro) and serve!
